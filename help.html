Rug Pull, also known as Exit Scamming is a malicious maneuver in the cryptocurrency industry where crypto developers abandon a project and disapear with investors’ capital. Rug pulls usually happen in the DeFi ecosystem, especially on decentralized exchanges (DEXs), where malicious individuals create a token and list it on a DEX, then pair it with a leading cryptocurrency only to build up some liquidity from unknowing investors and then they 'pull the rug' from under your feet and run off with your hard earned money hence the term. Some may also refer to these types of activities as 'exit scams' which is a synonymus term.

A DAAS or defi as a service is a service which offers to do the leg work for you while you invest and receieve X amount of rewards accoring to the proposal. Think of them as investment pots that groups of investors provide capital for in exchange for interest/profits on a daily/weekly or monthly basis.
